IBE Extensions

A LITTLE BIT ABOUT EXTENSIONS...


Invisible Bead Extensions will change your life! Whether you're seeking the confidence boost of fuller hair or looking to add several inches of length, Invisible Bead Extensions are the safest and most natural looking extension method available today! I have been certified in other hand tied extension methods and IBE blew me out of the water. With IBE you will not experience damage to your hair/scalp or any discomfort. What my clients do experience is growth and healthy hair. Once the hand tied extensions are in place, they look and feel completely natural. They can be colored, washed, curled, and styled just like natural hair. This flexibility, comfort and freedom makes hand tied extensions a top choice for many different lifestyles.
PLEASE NOTE - A 50% DEPOSIT IS DUE AT THE TIME OF BOOKING FOR ALL EXTENSIONS
